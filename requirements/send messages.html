Spread: Use Case #7

Title

Send Messages.

Actor

An account holder.

Prerequisite

The account holders need to be logged in and would have their own profile pages.

Action

An account holder would go to a message tab on another account holders profile page and send that user a typed message.

Effect

The account holder who had a message sent to them would be notified when they log in to their account

None.
