Spread: Use Case #8

Title

View inbox.

Actor

An account holder.

Prerequisite

The account holder will have to be logged in.

Action

The account holder will click on a button that will allow them to view an inbox.

Effect

They will be taken to a separate page for the inbox containing sent messages and received messages

Next

None.
