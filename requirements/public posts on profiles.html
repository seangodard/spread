Spread: Use Case #9

Title

Public posts on profiles.

Actor

An account holder.

Prerequisite

The account holder will have to be logged in.

Action

The account holder will click on a section of his/her profile page and that will allow them to type in a message.

Effect

The message the user writes in will be displayed on their profile page as a post.

None.
