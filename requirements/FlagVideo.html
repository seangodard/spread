Spread: Use Case #17

Title

Flag Video

Actors

Account Holder

Prerequisite

The user is on a page where an embedded video is present.

Action

The user clicks the Flag Video button, then selects one of the presented options in a new form that asks them why they flagged the video.

Effect

The video's flagged count increases by one. A message is sent to the Spread admins.

Next

No required follow-up actions, user continues watching the video or selects "Next Video" button when it becomes available.
