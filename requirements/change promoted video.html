Spread: Use Cases #6

Title

Change Promoted Video

Actor

An account holder

Prerequisite

The acount holder needs to be logged in to his/her account.

Action

The account holder needs to go to his or her settings then chooses an option that would say change promoted video. They will then be able to provide the link to the provided video.

Effect

This would change the video that is promoted to other viewers on the site.
