Spread: Use Case #10

Title

Edit and Delete Posts.

Actor

An account holder.

Prerequisite

The account holder will have to be logged in.

Action

The account holder will click on a section of his/her profile page and click a button to edit or delete a post.

Effect

Either the edited post will be displayed or the post will be deleted

None.
