Spread: Use Case #15

Title

Advance to Next Video

Actors

Account Holder

Prerequisite

The user must be present on their current tab for at least three fourths of the length of the current video that is displayed

Action

Once enough time has passed, the user then presses the Next Video button

Effect

The current page has 3 new videos added from semi-random registered users. The new videos are approximately the same length as the videos that the user has posted.

Next

Select and watch one of the new videos
