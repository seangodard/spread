Spread: Use Case #4

Title

Post Video

Actors

Account Holder

Prerequisite

The account holder must be logged in and provide a valid URL address.

Action

The account holder will input a URL address.

Effect

If the URL is valid, the video will be available to be sent out to other users on the site. If not, the account holder will be notified that an error occurred.
