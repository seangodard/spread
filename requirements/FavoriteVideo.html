Spread: Use Case #16

Title

Favorite Video

Actor

Account Holder

Prerequisite

The user is on a page where an embedded video is present.

Action

The user clicks on the Favorite Video button.

Effect

The video is added to the user's favorite videos list. The favorite count on the video is increased by one.

Next

No required follow-up actions.
