Spread

What's it called?

Spread

What is it?

Spread is a web application to provide up and coming content creators with a way to get their videos seen by others. To do this, you are asked to upload the link to a video you would like to promote. This video is then sent out to other users on the site. Each time you watch a video, your video is shared with another user. The bottom line is: the more videos you watch, the more your video is shared.

Who is it for?

Content creators without the funds to promote their videos in other ways and viewers looking for videos that would otherwise not making it into the public eye.
